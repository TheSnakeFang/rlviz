# Code of conduct

RolloutViz should be a useful, rigorous place to work on open tooling. Treat
contributors with respect, critique ideas and code rather than people, and make
room for different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained
personal attacks are not acceptable. Do not post private traces, credentials,
customer data, or another person's identifying information.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
needed to protect the project or its contributors. Report conduct issues
privately to the maintainers through GitHub. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical.
